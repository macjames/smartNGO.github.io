About Us

Smart NGO: Together we can save lives.


SmartNgo is more than just an organization; it's a movement fueled by a shared passion and a deep belief in the power of collective action to create positive change.
Our journey: From Inspiration to Impact
Our story began in 2024 when Mac James(CEO) witnesssed firsthand the Problems of Good healthy water and Education of Africans. Driven by driven by a project given by his teacher, he came together with a vision to Look for means to bring Clean water and Education to the people of Africa. What started as a small group of dedicated individuals has grown into a thriving network of passionate supporters, volunteers, and partners committed to making Africa better.

Our Mission and Values:

Smart NGO is dedicated to to building good educational structures, creating Scholarships and bringing good water to the Affected African villages. We believe in doing this
- Illiteracy rate will reduce
- Low sick rates at hospitals
- And making Africa more advance will bring revolution to our people. These values guide our actions and define our commitment to creating a more Developed, literate and healthy world.

What We Do:

Through programs, we are working to create clean water supplies in some minor and major vilages in African and the world, give out scholarships to the poor and uneducated and also building good educational structures. We are committed to achieving your mission, in empowering communities, providing essential services, advocating for change peacefully with the help of our partners and some individuals.
